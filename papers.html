PAPERS

Full papers are standard length papers (6-8 pages, ACM conference format) presenting novel research. All accepted full papers will be archived as conference proceedings and have a main session presentation at TAHRI.

Short papers are 2-4 pages and describe research that may be at any stage of development. The intent is to allow authors space to discuss & develop ideas with peers, and to receive constructive feedback on their experimental design to test technical components. Accepted short papers will be presented during a poster session at TAHRI.

REVIEW PROCESS

FULL PAPERS

The standard review process is designed for research that has not previously been reviewed elsewhere and thus needs to be thoroughly reviewed. It will use a standard process featuring three double-blind reviews and at least one meta review. Given the depth of the review, we plan for at least 5 weeks for the initial reviews to be completed.

The rapid review process is designed for research that has been submitted to another high quality research venue in the past 2 years. The review process will consist of two (meta) reviewers who will have two weeks to review the materials and propose a recommendation to the committee. Papers submitting an expedited review will be required to submit the following: Name of the previous venue Cover letter summarizing changes Original PDF + new PDF with annotated changes recommended with example instructions, both anonymized Reviews from previous venue Verification of previous submission: an email, that needs to include the name of the venue and name/contact details associated with that event (for example, the person sending the notification email) It is assumed that the author list will remain unchanged, but we require that at least one author from the original paper is also on the revised paper. Papers that were not accepted as part of the standard review (but received a resubmission recommendationwere highly rated?) will be allowed to resubmit their paper for the expedited review.

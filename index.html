International Symposium on Technological Advances for Human-Robot Interaction

ABOUT

The inaugural 2024 International Symposium on Technological Advances in Human-Robot Interaction (TAHRI 2024) invites technological HRI researchers to publish and gather to discuss technological HRI work in Boulder, Colorado.
The symposium provides a single event where researchers and industry partners can find the latest innovations and breakthroughs addressing challenges in creating, building, and improving human-robot interactions.

The symposium builds on the extensive community built during the ten years in which the AI-HRI symposium was hosted as part of the AAAI Fall Symposium Series. It also extends the community further by including technological advances beyond AI contributions.
We define "technological HRI" as practical hardware, software, actuators, sensors, mechanisms, algorithms, models, methods, interfaces, automations, frameworks, architectures, datasets, benchmarks, tools, techniques, or other technologies that can be directly incorporated into the design, development, or deployment of an HRI system.

TAHRI 2024 will include keynote sessions, contributed paper sessions, breakout discussions, and exhibitions from our corporate partners. To facilitate the broader dissemination of work that has been previously submitted to other venues, we provide an option for authors to submit revised work. In particular, we invite authors to submit to the AI-HRI and UR-RAD symposia and then extend their work for a submission to TAHRI.
